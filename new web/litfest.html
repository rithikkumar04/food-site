Mlr literary fest is an annual event that celebrates creativity and literature in all forms . We have slam poetry to give freedom to the words suffocating in your hearts and to express yourselves. Imagination has no boundaries , there is creative writing for the people who would love to pen thoughts, that makes you complete when you let it out on paper and to give shape to your imagination. Books are an escape from reality and there is Bookathon to immerse yourself in the world of imagination and let your creativity out. Photography is the only way to relive the moment we have photography to let you freeze a moment to reveal how rich reality could be. Treasure hunt to bring out the detectives inside you and excites you with every clue and to have lots of fun. And many more exciting events to escape you from the world of boredom and to bring out the creativity inside you to leave a mark.
